# Medical Emergencies

MedBook is a booking assistant, not an emergency service. It cannot diagnose,
prescribe, or advise on treatment. If you or someone with you is having a
medical emergency — chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, loss
of consciousness, stroke symptoms, or anything else that feels
life-threatening — do not wait for an appointment.

**Call 1122** (Pakistan's emergency ambulance service) or go directly to the
nearest hospital emergency room right away.

This applies regardless of how the symptoms are described — in English,
Urdu, or Roman Urdu — and regardless of whether a doctor's appointment was
already being discussed. Booking can always happen afterward, once the
emergency has been addressed.
